Project Snapshot

From Browsing Catalog to Purchase Confidence

The original store had strong traffic but weak conversion momentum. Users struggled to compare scents, understand bundle value, and decide quickly on mobile. The redesign focused on reducing decision fatigue and increasing checkout intent.

Product Architecture

Scent-Family Navigation

Products were grouped into clear scent families with visual tags and occasion labels so users could self-filter faster without opening every product page.

Bundle Strategy

Built-In Bundle Pathways

Prebuilt bundle cards were added around common buying intents like gifts, seasonal sets, and relaxation packs, reducing effort at decision points.

Purchase Confidence

Shipping and Burn-Time Clarity

Shipping windows, burn-time ranges, and scent profile confidence copy were moved above fold on mobile to remove hesitation before add-to-cart.

Launch System

Reusable Seasonal Template

A reusable launch structure lets the brand spin up holiday and seasonal campaigns quickly while preserving consistent conversion blocks.
