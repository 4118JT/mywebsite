Project Snapshot

From Generic Contact Form to Guided Intake System

The old form generated volume, not quality. The new quote system was built to collect key production details up front so the shop could estimate faster and reduce back-and-forth before approval.

Form Architecture

Guided Quote Builder

Intake now asks for garment type, stitch count range, order volume, due date, and artwork references in one structured flow.

Use-Case Routing

Intent-Led Entry Paths

Users choose pathways like school spirit wear, business uniforms, or team apparel so examples and options feel relevant immediately.

Confidence Layer

Visual Helper Blocks

Inline examples and plain-language helper copy reduce confusion for first-time buyers unfamiliar with embroidery terminology.

Operational Gain

Faster Quote Turnaround

Higher-quality inputs reduce internal clarification loops and let the team deliver accurate quotes much faster.
